Homer Uchiha

This website showcases Homer Uchiha, a unique fusion of Homer Simpson and the Uchiha clan from Naruto. The character features Homer’s signature shape and colors but with intense Sharingan eyes and an Akatsuki-style headband just like Itachi Uchiha. The background sets a dark and eerie tone forest that enhances the dramatic effect. This site serves as a fun and creative tribute to both The Simpsons and Naruto, blending two iconic worlds into a single website.

Schedule

This website helps me stay organized by giving me a clear view of my Normal Schedule and Advisory Schedule, so I always know where to be and when. It includes class times, room numbers, and subjects, making it easy to keep track of my day and avoid confusion. By having everything laid out in one place, I can quickly check my schedule without second-guessing myself. This makes it easier to manage my time and focus on my schoolwork without worrying about time.

My Recipe

This Tres Leches Cake website has a warm brown theme and features a History section detailing the cake’s origins and Nestlé’s role in its popularity. The Recipe section includes an Ingredients list with essentials like flour, eggs, and three types of milk, alongside Directions that provide a step-by-step guide to making the cake. A central image/video highlights the final product. The design is clean and structured with rounded content boxes and appealing visuals.
