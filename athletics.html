Athletics

Beyond coding, soccer is my second passion. I play as a Left Winger or Central Midfielder, and I thrive in high-paced, attacking plays. Dribbling past defenders, making key passes, and taking precise shots are what I do best. My go-to move? A quick change of direction to keep defenders on their toes. I love the creativity and strategy of the game—whether it’s setting up a perfect play or finding space where no one else sees it. Soccer isn’t just a sport for me; it’s a way to challenge myself, stay disciplined, and have fun competing.
I currently go to Lane Tech, and to be honest, school has always come naturally to me. I don’t study much, but I still manage to stay on top of everything. Instead of stressing over schoolwork, I prefer to spend my time on things that push me forward—whether it’s improving my game on the field, working on new web development projects, or just exploring new interests. I believe that growth comes from doing, not just learning, so I’m always looking for new challenges to take on.
When I’m not coding or playing soccer, I’m probably listening to music, watching games, or just hanging out and finding new things to get into. I love meeting people with similar interests, collaborating on projects, and constantly pushing myself to get better at whatever I do.
